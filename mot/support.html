Restoring a purchase

If you bought the full version and it isn't showing up, like on a new phone, open MOT and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so make sure you're signed in with the same one you used to buy. If it still doesn't restore, email us and we'll sort it out.

Your data and privacy

MOT works entirely on your device. There are no accounts and no servers, so your questions, readings, and journal never leave your phone. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
