Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MOT does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

The short version

MOT is a private space for reflection. It has no accounts, no login, and no network connection. The app never sends anything anywhere. We have no servers, so we never see your questions, your readings, or your journal.

What we collect

Nothing. MOT collects no personal information. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party data collection of any kind.

Your readings and journal

Any questions you ask, readings you receive, and journal entries you write are stored only on your own device. They are never uploaded, synced, or transmitted. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it. We have no copy and no way to recover it.

Purchases

MOT offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full app. This transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

MOT does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app contains no content collection, no communication features, and no external links to user-generated content.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll post the updated version on this page with a new date. Since MOT collects no data, we don't expect that to happen.
